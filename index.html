How I Troubleshoot Windows Network Issues in My Home Lab

Issue: no internet access inside a Windows VM. I checked IP settings, gateway, DNS, and adapter mode, then validated with ping and tracert. Fix: corrected virtual adapter settings and renewed IP. Lesson: always verify Layer 1-3 basics before advanced troubleshooting.

My Remote Support Checklist for Faster Ticket Resolution

Before solving a ticket, I confirm user impact, gather screenshots, check recent changes, and document error messages. During troubleshooting, I test one change at a time and confirm results with the user. This keeps support clear, calm, and faster.
